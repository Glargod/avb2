AVB²

Revolutionary Telemetry for Land, Sea, and Air Adventures

Introduction

The AVB² (All-Vehicle Black Box) is a compact, rugged, subscription-free telemetry device for adventure vehicles: ATVs, boats, snowmobiles, gliders, and future flying cars. It records ride logs and crash data—accessible via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth—with no cellular dependency. AVB² is the data logger and crash communicator; all visualizations, alerts, and enhanced features are delivered through the companion app.

The Challenge

  • No OBD-II ports in recreational or future flight vehicles
  • Current solutions depend on expensive subscriptions
  • Live signals fail in remote areas
  • Lack of standardized crash data access

The AVB² Solution

  • Universal Mount – Works with land, sea, and air platforms
  • Rugged Build – IP68 sealed, -40°C to 85°C range, ~100g crash resistance
  • Smart Logging – 10Hz GPS/IMU + crash buffer + event triggers
  • Private + Flexible – Wi-Fi syncing, Bluetooth crash access, optional cloud
  • App-Driven Features – Gamified air time, tilt logs, route visualization (via mobile app)
  • Affordable – No recurring fees, robust firmware
